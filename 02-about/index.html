Vast Hill is an Electric Synth duo from Sydney, Australia. Behind this dream-pop sound is Elle Kress and Aden Wilson. Vast Hill is the duo's homage to their favourite kinds of 80's Synthwave tunes with a heavy influence from 60s music.


Kicking off their project in 2015 with "Will You Love Me In The Rain", the duo continues to work on more material that will take you back to the 80s in a slick DeLorean.
